Our Main Project

Echoes of Darkness is a tactical card game blending the strategic depth of Gwent with the narrative soul of DnD.

In the forgotten corners of the realm, where the veil between worlds thins, ancient forces stir once more. Whispers echo through ruined spires, and cursed kings rise from their graves. You are not just a player— you are a commander of fate in a world where every card holds a secret, and every choice echoes in darkness.
